Green Towards Green

Who are we?

In recent years, more and more airlines have been offering their customers the option to “carbon offset” by paying an extra fee at the end of their plane ticket transactions. Supposedly, these funds go towards decarbonization initiatives meant to offset the passenger’s carbon footprint from that plane ride. However, controversy has arisen as to whether these profits are actually going towards their intended purposes.

Here at Green Towards Green, our goal is to ascertain for airline consumers where these funds are going for each airline company that offers them. Using our collection of databases, users can view where exactly their money is going, whether the extra fee is really worth it, and what airlines they may want to reconsider going to in the future. Here, we determine whether the green is really going towards green.
